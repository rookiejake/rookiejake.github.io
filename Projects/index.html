About

Welcome to the Cross Reality Collaboration Sandbox (CRCS) research group at the Computing and Software Systems Division of University of Washington Bothell.

Our team investigates how to support people working in collaboration addressing issues across realities where the solutions may span the continuum between physical and virtual spaces. One of the goals of our group is to facilitate the collaborations between participants that are located in a physical space of interests with participants that are located remotely.
