Augmented Space Library: Communicating via Virtual Space

By Justin Baker
April 2018

Abstract

The Cross Reality Collaboration Sandbox Research Group is focused on exploring mixed reality technologies. The goal of this research is to develop a framework, Augmented Space Library, that facilitates practical applications between users in different locations and using different devices.

Objectives

  • Add support for virtual reality devices to the Augmented Space Library (ASL)
  • Integrate VR support with existing portal system
  • Work with rest of research group to mix virtual room scanning, remote cameras, & RC car
  • Ease use of framework for future developers
