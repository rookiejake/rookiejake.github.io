Hello balloon

It's a simple balloon game. Use 'WASD' to control. The goal is to eat right fruit on the journey and arrive the end star. Pay attenion that the movement has inertia and not move out of the camera. We totally have four levels. When you win or lose, you can type your name and we will rank ans show your scores. Wish a good journey!
