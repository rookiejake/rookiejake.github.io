Winter 2018 CSS452: 2D Game Engine Development. Final Games

These are the final games we build during the last three weeks of class CSS452 in Winter Quarter 2018 as ways to demonstrate the features of the GTCS game engine we have learned to build in this class.

NOTE: You should run the games using the Chrome web browser. Explore 11 does not support AudioContext and Mozilla Firefox (v 39.0) does not support GLSL shaders and thus will not allow the games to run.

The Bouncy Adventures of BunBun The Bodacious Bunny

You are BunBun, and you are under attack by nasty aliens. You have to avoid being captured, but you also need the aliens to help you get to your destination. The aliens will try to stall you by turning your food (carrots) into dangerous rolling carrots. Get to the end of the level without being abducted or drowning.
