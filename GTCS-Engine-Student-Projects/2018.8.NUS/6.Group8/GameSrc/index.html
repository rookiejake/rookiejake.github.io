Behavior Controls:
  • D: Move rightward
  • B: Open/Close the bag
  • Num 1-4: Make the choice
  • Space: Close the dialog
  • Z: Give up the Adventure
Bag Controls:
  • Direction Keys: Move to choose the item
  • Enter: Use the item
  • T: Throw the item
