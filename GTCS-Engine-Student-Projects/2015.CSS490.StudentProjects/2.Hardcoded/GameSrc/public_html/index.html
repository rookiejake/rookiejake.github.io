CAVE ESCAPE!

A game by Team Hard Coded

Tyler Green and Steven Roberts

Controls

W + S = Move Up, Move Down

D + A = Speed Up, Slow Down (You start at maximum speed)

How to Play

Escape the Cave! Get as far as you can for a high score!

Bats, Rocks and Fire are all bad. Don't touch bad stuff!

Healing Drones are good: they heal you (yes, really). Touch good stuff!

Your SENSOR tells you about upcoming stuff. Keep an eye on it so you know what's coming!

Protip: Try slowing down when you get Fires and Rocks at the same time.
