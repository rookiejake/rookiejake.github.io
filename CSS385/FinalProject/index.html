General:

         Know yourself and your team: everyone should come clean and be honest:

o   Strength: Know what is your strengths (be modest, and be honest), and challenges. Make sure your team is aware of your strengths. E.g., Id suggest team member review each others MPs, and share your MP scores!

o   Commitment: Know what you can commit (in terms of hours per week), and make sure you deliver!

         Team work is important! You have to try to work together. However, if you really want to, team members can all decide to fire an unproductive member. But WATCH OUT!!

o   If you are fired by your team mates, you will work on final project by yourself. You will be graded by the same standard as everyone else.

o   If your team decides to fire a member, you will have one less person to contribute to your final game. Your team will be graded by the same standard as everyone else.

o   You see, it pays to try to work together!

         Responsibility and Participation are important!

o   A significant percentage of your demo grade will come from your peers (no less than 5%)!

o   After each demo, you must go on-line to submit your evaluation for your peers in a timely fashion (defined as: before the end of the week, by Sunday mid-night so that others can have time to act on the information you provide).

o   In addition, on each Sunday, you must go on-line to evaluate your own team (evaluation period: Monday to Sunday).

o   In all cases, you will receive email invitation to fill out survey and grade your peers. You will have till the end of that week (Sunday night at 11:45pm) to fill out the surveys!!

o   If you do not submit an evaluation for your peers or the evaluation of your team, you will not receive grades from your peers.

         Polished final product is important! A full 20% of your final project grade (about 2-4% of your entire class grade) depends on how polished your final project is. By polished we mean: bug free, plays well, and looks good.

         Final game ranking: After the final game demo, the class will vote and the winner will receive:

o   1st: GPA + 0.2

o   2nd: GPA + 0.1

o   3rd: GPA + 0.05
