Team Bionic Bard: Boss Turtle

Boss Turtle is a finite side-scrolling shooter defense game.

You beat the Bunny in a race, earning the eternal wrath of Bunnies worldwide. The player controls Boss Turtle who is defending its eggs from getting stolen by Bunnies.
